# Arus Digital — Homepage Copy Brief

> Lock this copy before touching HTML. Every section maps to the page architecture in REVIEW.md.
> Audience: Malaysian SME owners, founders, marketing managers, ops teams.
> Tone: plain, practical, local, confident. No hype, no heavy AI jargon.

---

## 1. Hero H1

**Primary option:**
Digital marketing, websites, apps and AI automation for Malaysian businesses.

**Fallback (shorter):**
We help Malaysian businesses grow online — with marketing, websites, apps and AI automation.

**Notes:**
- Lead with services + geography, not the company name.
- "Malaysian businesses" is preferred over "Malaysian SMEs" in the H1 so it reads naturally out loud.
- Avoid starting with "Arus Digital is..." or any AI-first framing.

---

Run Google Ads, Meta Ads and SEO campaigns, build corporate websites and ecommerce stores, develop web and mobile apps, and deploy WhatsApp automation and AI tools — all from a Kuala Lumpur team that works in BM and English.

**Notes:**
- Services named explicitly so the visitor scans quickly.

## Copy Rules for Developers

- Do not lead any section with the word "AI" in the heading. Services are practical outcomes first.
- Do not use the words "revolutionary", "transformative", "disruptive", or "cutting-edge".
- Do not reference specific LLM model names (Qwen, Nemotron, GLM) in homepage copy.
- "Malaysian SMEs" is fine in body copy but avoid it in H1/H2 where it can feel clinical.
- Every CTA button must have a WhatsApp link or anchor link as its href — no dead buttons.
- Prices: if shown, always in MYR, never USD.
- Trust section bullets: keep each to one short sentence. Do not overexplain.
