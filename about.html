Our Story

Built in KL.
Built for Malaysia.

Arus Digital started as a digital agency — helping Malaysian brands grow through digital marketing, web development, and software. That foundation is still who we are: a team that ships campaigns, builds products, and drives measurable growth for clients.

Along the way, we got obsessed with AI. Not as a buzzword — but as a real operating system for business. We turned that conviction on ourselves first: today, every internal function at Arus Digital runs through AI agents. Intelligence, development, research, strategy — all automated.

What we built to run ourselves became Miccy — an AI intelligence officer that delivers what every Malaysian CEO needs: the right information, before the window closes. Every morning. In your WhatsApp.

So today we're two things at once: a hands-on digital agency that grows your business, and an AI product company that automates it. Kami bantu anda besar, kemudian kami bantu anda automate.
