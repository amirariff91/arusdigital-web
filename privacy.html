Privacy Policy

How Arus Digital Sdn Bhd collects, uses, and protects your personal information — in plain language and in compliance with Malaysia's Personal Data Protection Act 2010.

Section 01

Who we are

Arus Digital Sdn Bhd is a digital agency and AI company based in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. We provide growth marketing, web development, software development, AI automation, and AI product services to Malaysian businesses.

For the purposes of Malaysia's Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA), Arus Digital Sdn Bhd is the data controller responsible for your personal information collected through this website and our services.

  • Company: Arus Digital Sdn Bhd
  • Location: Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia
  • Email: hello@arusdigital.com

Section 02

What data we collect

We only collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us — for example, when you fill out a contact form, send us an email, or reach out via WhatsApp. The types of data we may collect include:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • WhatsApp or phone number
  • Business name and industry
  • Any additional information you include in your message to us

We do not collect sensitive personal data (as defined under the PDPA) unless explicitly required and consented to for a specific service.

Section 03

How we use it

We use the personal information you provide solely for legitimate business purposes. Specifically:

  • To respond to your enquiries and provide quotations or proposals
  • To deliver the services you have engaged us for
  • To send service-related updates and communications
  • To improve our website and service offerings based on feedback
  • To comply with our legal and regulatory obligations

We will not use your personal data for unsolicited marketing without your prior consent. You may withdraw consent at any time by emailing us at hello@arusdigital.com.

Section 04

Who we share it with

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With cloud hosting and infrastructure providers necessary to operate our services (e.g. hosting, email delivery)
  • With analytics providers (e.g. Google Analytics) in anonymised or aggregated form
  • With professional advisors (legal, accounting) where required by law
  • When required by a court order, regulator, or government authority under Malaysian law

Any third-party service providers we engage are contractually required to handle your data in a manner consistent with this policy and applicable law.

✓ No data sold to third parties

Section 05

Data storage

Your personal data is stored on servers located in Malaysia and/or Singapore. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable Malaysian law and regulations. When data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted or anonymised.

✓ PDPA-compliant data handling

Section 06

Your rights

Under Malaysia's Personal Data Protection Act 2010, you have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right of correction — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to withdraw consent — withdraw your consent to processing at any time
  • Right to limit processing — request that we limit processing of your data in certain circumstances
  • Right of deletion — request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@arusdigital.com. We will respond to all requests within 21 days in accordance with the PDPA.

Section 07

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to understand how visitors interact with our site. We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4), which places cookies to collect anonymised usage data such as pages visited, time on site, and traffic sources.

  • Analytics cookies (GA4) — help us understand site usage and improve our content
  • No advertising or tracking cookies are used on this site
  • No cookies are used to identify you personally

You can disable cookies at any time through your browser settings. You may also opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. Disabling analytics cookies will not affect your ability to use this website.
